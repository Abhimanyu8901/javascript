About Blood Saathi

Blood Saathi is a dedicated platform connecting blood donors with those in need. With the motto "जरूरत पर साथ" (Companion in Time of Need), we bridge the gap between life-saving donations and urgent requirements across communities.

Our Mission

❤️

Save Lives

Every donation saves up to 3 lives. We make it easy to give blood and find donors.

🌐

Connect Communities

Building a network of donors, hospitals, and patients for faster, reliable blood access.

📈

Increase Awareness

Educating on blood donation benefits, myths, and the critical need for regular donors.
